Q. Who or which Union Ministry launches NIPUN Bharat Programme?

Answer: Union Minister for Education, Ramesh Pokhriyal

Note: Union Education Minister launches NIPUN Bharat Programme with of to cover the learning needs of children in the age group of 3 to 9 years. NIPUN stands for National Initiative for Proficiency in Reading with Understanding and Numeracy.

Q. ভারতের কোন অর্থনীতিবীদ Humboldt Research Awards পেলেন?
